Chalkboard TicTacToe

Chalkboard Tic-Tac-Toe is a web version of the classic two-player paper and pencil game. It must be played on two screens simultaneously, so find a friend and begin playing! The game was built with HTML5, CSS3, CSS animations, AngularJS, and Firebase.

College Organizer

Applying to college can be overwhelming. College Organizer hopes to make the whole process a little easier by tracking the colleges about which students feel confident and those they don't. Students can search through the top 370+ colleges and universities in the country, view important admissions criteria particular to each college, and add the schools to their Safety, Target, and Reach lists in an effort to keep track of their searches and stay organized. The app was built using Ruby on Rails and MongoDB and is deployed via Heroku.

Geomancy

Geomancy is an exciting music discovery app that allows users to explore popular new music around the globe. When a user clicks on one of 50+ cities from around the globe, Geomancy loads a playlist of the most popular artists in that city. When the user plays a track, Geomancy populates a feed of the latest tweets that tag the artist. It utilizes three APIs (WebGL, Soundcloud, and Twitter), was built using HTML5, CSS3, Rails, AngularJS, JavaScript, jQuery, and PostgreSQL, and it is deployed via Heroku.
